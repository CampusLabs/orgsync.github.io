Our Team

We are a small team of developers and designers commited to building a robust, performant and scalable web application that our users love. Most of our code is written in Ruby and runs on the Rails stack backed by a MySQL database, but we also incorporate other technologies such as Node.js, Memcached and Redis. We use GitHub for our version control and our infrastructure is entirely hosted via cloud services.

Our team is a bunch of badasses. You should consider joining it. We're looking for good people. If you have any questions email us: devteam@orgsync.com
