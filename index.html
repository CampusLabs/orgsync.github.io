How we get work done

We believe that meetings suck. We have a daily standup for < 10 minutes and weekly agile-like planning session to break down development tasks.

We deploy all the time. Everyone deploys.

We communicate with HipChat. It’s a constant stream of dev talk, site stats and animated gifs.

Most projects have ad-hoc teams that will collaborate however they see fit. We endorse pair programming, collaborative learning, sharing ideas and trying something new.
